# Ubika Child Safety Standards

Ubika is committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users.

We strictly prohibit content that involves child sexual abuse or exploitation (CSAE).

## Safety Measures

- Users can report alerts, content, or other users directly within the app.
- Reported content is reviewed and removed if it violates our policies.
- Accounts sharing illegal or harmful content are permanently banned.
- Ubika cooperates with law enforcement when legally required.

## Reporting Concerns

If you encounter content that violates these standards, please report it using the reporting tools available in the application.
